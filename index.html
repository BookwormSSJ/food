BUTTER CHICKEN

DESCRIPTION

Butter chicken, traditionally known as murgh makhani (pronounced [mʊrɣ ˈməkʰaːni]), is an Indian dish originating in Delhi.[1][2] It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste

  • 1 ¾ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder, divided
  • salt to taste
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garam masala
